59 Points of Intimacy Notes

Details

What does it mean to shift our attention from the thing to the connection between things, from the subjects to the threads? I took some notes on different points of intimacy, from a haircut to seeing a kid drop their toy. This also ended up being an exercise in poetic observation, to see what is there, but to see beyond what is there too. A drop can be a kiss; a mundane routine can be an elegant dance.

On its surface, there is an appeal to applying this lens towards the world, it brings an amount of whimsy and artfulness to the everyday. But I think there actually ends up being a bit of utility to drawing these connections and pulling at these threads. It's the use of creativity to shape a new reality, in the most elemental and basic way. And as with most things, it's something that gets better with time and with repetition, so seeing a chocolate stain and seeing intimacy becomes a key exercise to better understanding a reality of interconnectedness. And interconnectedness, that's what it's always been about.
